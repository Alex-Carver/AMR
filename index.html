Welcome to AMR

Advanced Midwest Radiology (AMR) is a professional service corporation dedicated to providing a full spectrum of consultative diagnostic and interventional radiology services to our partners. The range of expertise includes board certified/fellowship trained onsite and offsite diagnostic interpretations, on-site interventional procedures, medical directorship, guidance with professional society accreditations, as well as integrating the latest developments in healthcare quality and patient safety into the imaging stream to maximize value. AMR has been providing services to the southwest suburbs of Chicago since the 1960's and officially incorporated in 1971. For more information about our services, contact us at (815) 300-7303.
